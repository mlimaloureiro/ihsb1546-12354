How can you make a difference ?

Say it affects you

You can say reports created by others affect you and you'll automatically be able to get notifications about them, check their current state and know updates about them. The more affected people one report has, the more it gets noticed by entities.

Contribute

Do you feel affected by something that's wrong around you? Probably others feel it too. Contribute to fix them by reporting those problems and engaging people to say it affects them too.

PRO

For entities responsible for the management of occurrences our users are reporting, contact us to acquire the PRO account and get live analytics, monthly reports, and decision support systems to help you solve problems.
